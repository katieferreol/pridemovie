KATIE FERREOL

Katie is an incoming sophomore from Manila, Philippines. She just recently declared her major in Interactive Media, also while doing a minor in Film & New Media and Economics. She plays the role of "Katie", Pam's lover, in the short film.

The view of the LGBTQ+ community in the Philippines has been strong since the beginning, but it is steadily growing to be more accepting. Because majority of the nation is predominantly Catholic, LGBTQ+ people in the Philippines have limited legal rights, such as no same-sex marriage. However, with the Philippines having a wide array of celebrities such as Vice Ganda––a gay talk show host and actor––making history in popular media, Filipinos are growing out of their conservative mindsets.
