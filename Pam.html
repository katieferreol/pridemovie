PAM BARRERA

Pam is an incoming sophomore from Venezuela. She is currently majoring in Film & New Media, while also taking classes in both Economics and Interactive Media to gain additional skills and expertise. She plays the role of "Pam", Katie's lover, in the short film.

The Venezuelan view on the LGBTQ+ community is not as open as it is in Europe or in the United States. Both male and female same-sex sexual activity is legal in Venezuela, but same-sex couples and households headed by same-sex couples are not eligible for the same legal protections available to opposite-sex married couples. Because Venezuela is predominantly Catholic, this made it difficult to cater to both religious and accepting perspectives.
