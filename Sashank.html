SASHANK SILWAL

Sashank is an incoming sophomore from Kathmandu, Nepal majoring in both Computer Science and Interactive Media. It is interesting to note that those from Nepal are able to understand both Hindi and Urdu. He plays the role of "Sashank", Pam's friend, in the short film.

Nepal has demonstrated its immense progress in accepting the LGBTQ+ community. The Nepalese Constitution recognizes and treats LGBTQ+ rights as fundamental rights, treating them as they should be. The Nepali Government, following the end of the monarchy, legalized homosexuality across the country in 2007 along with the introduction of several new laws. These new laws explicitly include protections on the basis of sexual orientation.
