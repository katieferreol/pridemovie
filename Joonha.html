JOONHA YU

Joonha is an incoming sophomore from South Korea. He lived in Indonesia since he was young and is currently majoring in Computer Science. He plays the role of "Joonha", Pam's and Katie's inner voices, in the short film.

Korea's view on the LGBTQ+ community is mixed depending on generations. The young generations are more accepting, shown by the multitude of LGBTQ+ friendly locations such as Hongdae and Seoul. However, while male and female same-sex sexual activity is legal in South Korea, marriage or other forms of legal partnership are not available to same-sex partners. Homosexuality in South Korea is not mentioned in either the South Korean Constitution or in the Civil Penal Code.
